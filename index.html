Ratings Now!

"Ratings Now!" helps anyone find the best ratings for movies and television shows before deciding what to watch. Simply point your phone camera at your TV and tap on a title when you're trying to decide on a show or movie from your streaming service list and instantly see the ratings from your favorite ratings provider. Alternatively use our voice mode to announce titles for instant ratings from trending and popular shows and movies.
